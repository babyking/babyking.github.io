## Pilot ASC App Support

This page provides app support information and key links.

### App Overview
ASCFlow is a powerful automation tool designed for iOS developers to streamline App Store Connect metadata management. It automates the entire workflow from metadata preparation to submission, saving you hours of manual work.

Key Features:
• Automated metadata management for 33 languages
• One-click IAP configuration and pricing updates
• Batch screenshot upload with device frame matching
• Smart validation to prevent submission errors
• Git-based workflow integration for team collaboration

Who is it for:
Perfect for indie developers, small studios, and agencies managing multiple apps. Whether you publish one app or fifty, ASCFlow scales with your needs.

Why choose ASCFlow:
Unlike manual ASC management or generic tools, ASCFlow understands the specific requirements of App Store submissions. It enforces compliance rules automatically and keeps your metadata consistent across all locales.

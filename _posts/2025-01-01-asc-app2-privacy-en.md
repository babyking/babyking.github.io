## ASC App2 Privacy Policy

ASC App2 is a validation app for a reusable App Store Connect submission workflow.
This app itself does not provide account registration or independent cloud account services.

### Data and Privacy
- We do not collect personal identity information for marketing purposes.
- If system logs are generated by Apple platform services, they are used only for debugging and reliability analysis.
- We do not sell user data to third parties.

### In-App Purchase
- If a subscription or purchase is offered, payment is charged through your Apple account.
- Subscription renewal and cancellation are managed by Apple.

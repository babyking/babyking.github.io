## BatchConvert

BatchConvert is the ultimate all-in-one file conversion tool for iOS. Convert audio, video, images, eBooks, and documents with ease. Whether you need to convert MP3 to WAV, MP4 to AVI, JPG to PNG, or EPUB to PDF, our powerful converter handles it all.

Key Features:
	•	Batch Conversion: Convert multiple files simultaneously with queue management. Save time by processing hundreds of files at once.
	•	Audio Converter: Support for MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG, M4A, WMA with adjustable bitrate and sample rate. Fast local processing with FFmpeg.
	•	Video Converter: Support for MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, FLV, WMV, WEBM, GIF with multiple resolution options (480p, 720p, 1080p, 4K). Video to GIF conversion optimized for iOS devices.
	•	Image Converter: Support for JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, BMP, SVG, HEIC, HEIF with quality adjustment and batch resize. Perfect for web and social media.
	•	eBook Converter: Support for EPUB, MOBI, AZW3, PDF, TXT, DOCX. Preserve formatting and layout, perfect for Kindle and other readers.
	•	AI-Powered Audio Separator: Extract vocals from music, isolate instruments (drums, piano, bass), create karaoke tracks with professional-grade AI separation.
	•	Local & Cloud Processing: Fast local conversion for audio/video, cloud processing for complex tasks.
	•	iCloud Integration: Access files from iCloud Drive with seamless file management.
	•	File Manager: Organize and manage converted files with easy sharing to other apps.

Whether you're a content creator, musician, photographer, student, or professional, BatchConvert makes it easy to convert any file format instantly. Download now and experience the most powerful file converter on iOS!

Content creators, video editors, musicians, audio engineers, photographers, designers, students, professionals, and anyone who works with digital files.

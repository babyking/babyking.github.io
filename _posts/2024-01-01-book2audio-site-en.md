## Book2Audio

Book2Audio is a powerful eBook to audiobook converter designed for book lovers and busy professionals. Transform your favorite eBooks into high-quality audiobooks and enjoy them anytime, anywhere.

Key Features:
	•	Multi-Format Support: Convert EPUB, MOBI, PDF, and other popular eBook formats to MP3 audiobooks.
	•	Smart Text Processing: Skip copyright pages, prefaces, and other non-content sections with intelligent start position selection.
	•	Flexible Splitting Options: Split your audiobook by chapters, line count, or audio size to fit your preferences.
	•	High-Quality TTS: Choose from 330+ natural-sounding voices in multiple languages powered by advanced neural TTS technology.
	•	Offline Playback: Listen to your converted audiobooks offline with a built-in audio player featuring playback controls and progress tracking.
	•	Batch Conversion: Convert multiple segments or entire books efficiently with queue management.

Whether you're commuting, exercising, or relaxing, Book2Audio makes it easy to enjoy your favorite books in audio format. Download now and start listening!


Applicable scene:
Commuters, fitness enthusiasts, visually impaired users, students, professionals, and anyone who prefers listening to reading.
